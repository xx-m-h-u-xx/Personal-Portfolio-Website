Who Am I ? What do I do?

A calm-cooling, easy-going indivdual , Healal is a comfortable IT literate. Born on the year 2000, the year in which computers have started to revolutionise, he has been intrigued by the development of systems and technologies since at an early age during his GCSEs years. Commenced in September 2018, he is studying BSC Computer Science in Manchester Metropolitan University, having enrolled successfully and gained a place to this course in the Summer of 2018. A winner and a participant of the Design Ventura 2014 - Healal had been nominated for a year-long design competition with 4 other peers, representing the school. Healal and his team originated an initial design idea to a constructed packaged product competiting against 47,000 other students. Collectively as a team, they launched their very own designed product in the Design Museum Shop, London.
